DEVELOPERS OPEN APPLICATIONS

We are currently looking for people to join our development team for this game! If you have the skills in the following categories and are interested in helping us out, feel free to join our Discord and give PurpleBanette a ping, or send a contact form informing us that you want to join the team! Note: This is volunteer for a gaming project, not a job.


Openings


3D-Modeler


Skills Required:

  1. Creating low poly game assets such as objects, characters, creatures, levels, or machines.
  2. Retopologizing high res 3D models into low poly assets.
  3. Rigging models for use in-game.
  4. Knowledge of how to export models properly into Unreal Engine 4.
  5. Creativity.

Pluses:

  1. Modeling futuristic armor or robots/drones, there’s going to be A LOT of them.
  2. Interest in the Sci-Fi genre.

3D-Animator

Skills Required:

  1. Understanding human/creature anatomy and body language to be able to animate characters in creative ways that express their personalities and behaviors.
  2. Animating other assets such as objects, machines, or creatures.
  3. Knowledge of how to export animations properly into Unreal Engine 4.
  4. Creativity.

Pluses

  1. Interest in the Sci-Fi genre.
  2. Knowledge of how cloth physics, hair physics, and particles work.

Concept Artist

Skills Required:

  1. Able to create clear visual images of ideas such as characters, creatures, weapons, machines, or environments.
  2. Creating reference sheets with enough information to be able to effectively turn concepts into 3D models.
  3. Knowledge of anatomy to create concepts for characters/creatures.
  4. Creativity.

Pluses

  1. Interest in the Sci-Fi genre.
  2. Creating concepts for futuristic armor or robots/drones, there’s going to be A LOT of them.
  3. Understanding the universe of this game and its limitations in order to create concepts that will fit in that universe.

Engine Programmer

Skills Required:

  1. Knowledge of coding in Unreal Engine 4.
  2. Knowledge of coding in the C++ language.
  3. Knowledge of Git and how Gitlab pipelines work.
  4. Creativity.
  5. Communication skills.

Pluses:

  1. Interest in the Sci-Fi genre.

3D Texture Painter

Skills Required:

  1. Knowledge of how textures and materials work.
  2. Knowledge of Physically-based rendering (PBR) and how to make PBR textures.
  3. Mapping 3D models to be textured.
  4. Creating textures for characters, creatures, objects, environments, weapons, or machines.
  5. Creativity.
  6. Knowledge of how Unreal Engine 4 handles textures.

Pluses:

  1. Interest in the Sci-Fi genre.

Story Writer

Skills Required:

  1. Knowledge of how to portray character personalities, interests, and interactions depending on the situations they are in.
  2. Knowledge of how to create character development throughout the course of a story.
  3. Knowledge of how to progress an overall story.
  4. Skills in communication and problem solving.
  5. Creativity.

Pluses:

  1. Interest in the Sci-Fi genre.
  2. Knowledge of Biology and Genetics.
  3. Understanding the universe of this game and its limitations in order to come up with ideas that will fit in that universe.
